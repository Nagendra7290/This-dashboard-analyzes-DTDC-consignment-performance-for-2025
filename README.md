🚚 **End-to-End Logistics Performance Analysis | Business Intelligence | Power BI**

This project delivers a **comprehensive analytical dashboard** for **DTDC’s monthly consignment operations in 2025**, providing clear visibility into **shipment volume, revenue trends, regional performance, transport modes, customer behavior, and payment preferences**.  
The dashboard enables **data-driven decision-making** for logistics, operations, and supply chain management teams.

---

## 📌 Project Objective

To analyze **monthly consignment data (June & July 2025)** and:
- Identify demand patterns and revenue trends
- Evaluate operational efficiency across states
- Understand customer and payment behavior
- Support strategic planning and optimization

---

## 🧾 Dataset Overview

The dataset includes:
- Booking Month & Date
- Sender State
- Total Pieces
- Total Amount
- Mode of Transport
- Nature of Consignment (Dox / Non-Dox)
- Customer Relationship Type
- Mode of Payment

---

## 📈 Month-Wise Performance Analysis

| Month | Total Pieces | Revenue Contribution |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| **June** | 124K (83.4%) | ~₹21M |
| **July** | 25K (16.6%) | ~₹4M |

### 🔍 Insights
- June is the **primary revenue and volume driver**
- July shows a **significant drop (~80%)** in consignments
- Indicates **seasonality, operational constraints, or partial month data**
- Highlights the need for **better monthly demand balancing**

---

## 🚚 Mode of Transport Distribution

| Transport Mode | Share |
|---------------|-------|
| **Surface** | ~60% |
| Express | ~20% |
| Air Cargo | ~20% |

### 🔍 Insights
- Surface transport dominates due to **cost efficiency**
- Express & Air Cargo together form **40%**, indicating strong demand for faster deliveries
- Opportunity to **upsell premium logistics services**

---

## 🗺️ State-Wise Performance Analysis

### 🏆 Top Revenue Generating States
1. Maharashtra  
2. Uttar Pradesh  
3. Andhra Pradesh  
4. Gujarat  
5. Tamil Nadu  

### 🔍 Insights
- Metro and industrial states contribute the **highest revenue and shipment volumes**
- Strong correlation between **shipment count and revenue**
- Scope to improve penetration in **mid-performing and emerging states**

## 📦 Nature of Consignment

|-----|-------|

### 🔍 Insights

---

## 💳 Mode of Payment Analysis

| Payment Mode | Share |
|-------------|-------|
| **Cash** | ~60% |
| Card | ~36% |
| Wallet | ~4% |

### 🔍 Insights
- Cash remains dominant, increasing handling and reconciliation effort
- Digital payments show **strong growth potential**
- Wallet usage is minimal → opportunity for **incentives & offers**

---

## 📌 Strategic Business Insights

✔ Revenue is **volume-driven**, not price-driven  
✔ Strong dependence on a single month (June)  
✔ Digital payment adoption can reduce operational costs  
✔ Premium delivery services have expansion potential  
✔ State-wise targeting can improve overall performance
